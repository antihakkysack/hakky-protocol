# Hakky Protocol — 2-Week Launch Content Calendar

Sequenced along the `[[customer-awareness-timeline]]`: Week 1 moves a cold audience from **problem-aware** (freezes are real, and invisible) to **solution-aware** (portable, on-chain cleanliness exists). Week 2 goes deep, handles objections, and converts the **Hakky-aware** into followers, readers, and repo-stargazers. Every post keeps the holder as hero and Hakky as guide (`[[storybrand-framework]]`, `[[guide-positioning]]`).

**Cadence:** 1 anchor post/day + 2–4 replies/quote-tweets in-community. Times are placeholders — align to when your audience (US + EU crypto hours) is awake. Handle: **@antihakkysack**.

**Legend:** 🎯 awareness stage · 📌 = pin candidate.

## WEEK 1 — Problem → Solution

- **Anchor:** Short thread (3–4 tweets): *"How a 'clean' Bitcoin market quietly formed."* Frozen deposits, refused withdrawals, the OTC premium on virgin coins. No solution yet — just name the pain.

- **Anchor:** Publish the full launch thread (`twitter-launch-thread.md`). **Pin Tweet 1.** This is the week's centerpiece.

### Day 5 (Fri) — "What Hakky is NOT" 🎯 Solution-aware

### Day 6 (Sat) — Community / lighter engagement 🎯 mixed

## Running rules for the fortnight
- **One idea per post.** Threads for depth, singles for punch.
- **Reply > broadcast.** Budget time daily to answer questions in-thread — it compounds trust and reach.
- **Never post value/price talk.** Add "not financial advice / DYOR" whenever a post touches worth or holdings.
- **Guard the category.** If anyone frames Hakky as a mixer/privacy tool, correct it politely and immediately.
- **Repurpose.** Each deep-dive thread → a short graphic → a one-line quote. Same idea, three formats.
- **Scam watch.** Proactively warn about fake "presale"/airdrop DMs; you have no token sale.
