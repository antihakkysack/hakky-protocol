# Hakky Protocol — Twitter/X Launch Thread

**Goal:** Stop the scroll, teach the problem, present cBTC + attestations as the fix, kill the "mixer/surveillance" objection head-on, and close with a clear, hype-free CTA.

**Doctrine applied:** hook in the first beat (`[[power-triangle-framework]]`); customer (the honest BTC holder) is the hero and Hakky is the guide — empathy then authority (`[[storybrand-framework]]`, `[[guide-positioning]]`); the thread walks a cold reader from problem-aware → solution-aware → Hakky-aware (`[[customer-awareness-timeline]]`).

**Usage notes:**
- Char counts below are approximate — paste each into X and confirm it sits under 280 before posting (emoji count as 2 chars).
- Emoji used sparingly, hashtags only where they read naturally (final tweet).
- Post as a single connected thread. Pin Tweet 1 after publishing (see `bio-and-assets.md`).

## Why this thread is built this way
- **Tweet 1 does one job:** stop the scroll with a concrete, personal stake (your coins, frozen, no fault of yours). Curiosity gap, not a feature list — `[[power-triangle-framework]]`.
- **Tweets 2–3** establish the external problem (freezes/premium) and the internal problem (the anxiety of not being able to see it) before offering a solution — `[[storybrand-framework]]`.
- **Tweets 4–6** position Hakky as the guide: empathy first, then authority via a concrete mechanism (cBTC, attestations, PoR) — `[[guide-positioning]]`.
- **Tweets 7–9** pre-empt the two objections that would otherwise kill trust (mixer? surveillance? really trustless?). Handling them unprompted is itself a credibility signal.
- **Tweets 10–11** stay hype-free by design — the honesty *is* the differentiator with a crypto-native, burned-before audience.
